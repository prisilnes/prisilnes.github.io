Hey! I’m Prisilia Ines, the artist behind Pineswood.id. I’m a web developer and self-taught Illustrator. The name Pineswood itself stands for Prisilia Ines World Of Design, showing the love I have for design, especially chubby and cute characters. My interest is in improving my skills and to learn new techniques to create better illustrations. One of my dreams is to share my happiness through my artwork. Therefore, Pineswood main motto is There’s no happiness without Pines.
